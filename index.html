A word from Dr. Dillemans

Dr. Dillemans and the BARIAlink team are delighted to invite you to celebrate the 10-Year Anniversary of BARIAlink with us.

Over the past decade, BARIAlink has served as a dedicated platform for the discussion of complex and challenging cases in metabolic bariatric surgery, fostering collaboration and sharing expertise and clinical excellence.

On this special occasion, we will reflect on some of our BARIAlink achievements, but more importanly focus on the future, both in further improving our educational goals as well as keeping an open view on the still rapidly evolving field of metabolic bariatric surgery and other treatment modalities.

We look forward to welcoming you for an engaging and insightful gathering.
